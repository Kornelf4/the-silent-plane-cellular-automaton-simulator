Controls:
Click anywhere to toggle a cell.
Use WASD keys to move the camera.
Use the wheel or the buttons to zoom in or out.
Use space to pause/start the simulation.
Press c and click a cell to select and modify it.
Click on it once again (while pressing c) to deselect it, and go to default mode.
If you press c and click a dead cell, you can view (and modify) the dead cell settings.
You can also go to dead cell settings if you press h.
You can go to default mode if you deselect a cell, or press n.
